AIESEC Product

oGTa

Global Talent is an AIESEC program designed to connect young people with professional internship opportunities abroad. Participants gain valuable international work experience, improve their skills, and enhance their employability while working in companies or organizations in different countries. The program focuses on providing real-world experience in various fields such as marketing, business development, IT, finance, and engineering.

  • Product: Outgoing Global Talent
  • Target: Youth aged 18 to 30

AIESEC Product

oGTe

Global Teacher is a program by AIESEC that offers young people the opportunity to teach abroad, focusing on improving education in underserved communities. Participants teach English ,French,Music or other subjects in schools, gaining valuable teaching experience while immersing themselves in a new culture. The program promotes leadership development, cultural exchange, and social impact by addressing educational inequalities and enhancing access to quality education. It's an enriching experience for those passionate about education and making a positive difference in the world.

  • Product: Outgoing Global Teacher
  • Target: Youth aged 18 to 30
